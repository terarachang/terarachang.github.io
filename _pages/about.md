My name is Ting-Yun Chang (張婷雲). I am now a research assistant in Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica, Taiwan, under the supervision of [Dr. Chi-Jen Lu](https://www.iis.sinica.edu.tw/pages/cjlu/). My research interest includes deep learning for natural language processing and computer vision.

I received my BSc degree from the Department of Computer Science at National Tsing Hua University and my MSc degree from the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taiwan University, working with [Dr. Yun-Nung (Vivian) Chen](https://www.csie.ntu.edu.tw/~yvchen/). I was an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon Alexa AI, California.
